How to Calculate the Circumference of a Circle

Formula: C = 2πr

The circumference (C) of a circle can be calculated using the formula:

C = 2πr

Where:

  • C is the circumference
  • π is approximately 3.14159
  • r is the radius of the circle

How to calculate the perimeter of a square:

To find the perimeter of a square, you simply need to multiply the length of one side by 4.

Here's the formula:

  • Perimeter = side length * 4

For example:

If a square has a side length of 5 cm, its perimeter would be:

  • Perimeter = 5 cm * 4 = 20 cm

So, the perimeter of the square is 20 cm.
